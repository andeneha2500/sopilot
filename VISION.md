## SOPilot — AI-Powered SOP Generator

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** May 2026

---

## Product Name
SOPilot

## Problem Statement
Business teams waste hours writing Standard Operating Procedures manually. The process is inconsistent, often skipped, and hard to maintain. There is no quick way to go from "I know this process" to a professional, structured document.

## Who Uses It (Actors)
- Operations Managers
- HR Teams
- Business Analysts
- Any team lead who owns a process

## Core Workflow
1. User describes a business process in plain English
2. User selects department context (HR, Finance, Operations, IT, Sales)
3. User sets complexity level (Simple / Detailed)
4. AI generates a full SOP with: Title, Purpose, Scope, Roles & Responsibilities, Step-by-step procedure, Edge Cases, and Success Metrics
5. User can refine by giving a follow-up instruction
6. User downloads the SOP as a PDF

## Success Metrics
- SOP generated in under 30 seconds
- Output is structured, professional, and ready to use
- Works for any business domain without configuration
